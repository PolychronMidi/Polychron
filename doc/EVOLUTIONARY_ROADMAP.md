# Evolutionary Roadmap

> Post-R32 deep audit synthesis. Three research agents audited conductor intelligence, cross-layer emergence topology, and composer/play self-awareness across 473 source files.

## Three Intelligence Ceilings

### Ceiling 1: No Compositional Memory

The system forgets what it just did. Composers don't see what was actually emitted after modifiers. No pattern histogram detects saturation. Cross-section memory stores state but never evaluates quality.

**Breakthroughs:**
- Emission accountability: return summaries to composers (selected vs emitted)
- Motif pattern histograms: detect and deprioritize overused transforms
- Section quality scoring: feed forward into next section's intent targets

### Ceiling 2: No Global Emergence

Modules are locally smart, globally blind. 5 dead-end L0 channels (articulation, grooveTransfer, phase, spectral, registerCollision). Trust payoffs reward individual outcomes, not emergent combinations. No convergence momentum.

**Breakthroughs:**
- Wire dead-end L0 channels into consumers
- Add channels: climax-pressure, texture-sustain, density-rhythm, rest-sync
- Convergence momentum: rapid convergences lower threshold (system learns its own rhythm)
- Emergence bonus in trust payoff: multiplicative reward for coordinated multi-system events

### Ceiling 3: No Self-Narration

60+ observation dimensions but no causal chains. System detects symptoms not causes. Coupling labels describe states not trajectories. No mid-composition self-evaluation.

**Breakthroughs:**
- Temporal coupling labels: building/dissolving/oscillating trajectories
- Causal chain recording: link events to their triggers
- Mid-composition self-evaluation: assess first half, adjust second half goals
